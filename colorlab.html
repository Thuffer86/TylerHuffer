Color Lab

CSS

Open Source

Color Lab was the first open source project I ever created on Github. After spending time studying Google's material design guidelines, I wanted to use their design patterns to create something useful. So in the spirit of design I created a tool to make finding material design colors, quick and easy.


The pallete includes over 100 colors that can be one-click copied and then pasted seamlessly into your code or design. It's especially helpful for me when I'm designing websites. The tool helps me to find colors I like and paste them into my SASS/LESS variables.
