Who I am

Hi, I'm Tyler Huffer. I am a highly motivated and committed UI designer and front end developer. I have a passion for creating connections between people and brands.


I've been creating websites professionally for over 4 years. I am able to code websites from scratch - start to finish. I am also proficient in Adobe Creative Suites as well as many other design tools and libraries. I'm also currently available to take on new projects!


Although I enjoy front end development and UI design because of the freedom I have to paint my vision, I'm also effective at assessing my clients desires and designing an experience that matches their personal expectations as well as their audience.

What I do

I am a web designer and front end developer. I excel at HTML, CSS, Javascript, Adobe, Bootstrap, Shopify, and Wordpress. I can create entire websites as well as single page web applications. Some other technical skills of mine include responsive design, google dev tools, SEO, and I am proficient with essential linux command line tools notably git.


I have over 4 years of professional experience designing and developing websites, client-side SPA's, graphics, wordpress, emails, and interfaces. Contact me if you're looking for a developer or a designer.

Creative Services

Web Design is a multi step process that begins with finding out not only who you are, but also who your audience is. It is important that we keep in mind the user, as well as staying true to the brand personality. Once we answer these questions I can build an experience.

Web Development- A great design is more than what's on the surface. I create clean and maintainable code built on years of experience. The code is the foundation of a website and a strong foundation is important. I can develop websites without a CMS or framework, but I also have extensive experience with Wordpress.

Branding & SEO- Part of the development process is getting your website seen. I do research to identify converting keywords based on many factors. I will even install and configure Google Analytics so that we can easily discover who your site visitors are, and what they are doing.
